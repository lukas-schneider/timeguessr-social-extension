# Privacy Policy for TimeGuessr Social Extension

**Last Updated:** 2026-08-02

## Data We Collect

This extension collects and transmits the following information to our backend servers:

### User Information

- **User ID**: A randomly generated unique identifier (UUID) used to distinguish users
- **User Initials**: Your chosen display initials for leaderboards and social features
- **Activity Data**: Your gameplay data from timeguessr.com, including scores and game results

## How We Use Your Data

- **Leaderboards**: Display your initials and scores on social leaderboards
- **Social Features**: Enable multiplayer and community features
- **Service Operation**: Maintain and improve the extension's functionality

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is transmitted securely to our servers
- Your User ID is randomly generated and not linked to any personal information
- We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information beyond what you explicitly provide (initials)

## Third-Party Access

- We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties
- Data is only used within the TimeGuessr Social Extension ecosystem
